About IFELAW

The Law Students’ Society is the umbrella body of law students at the faculty of law Obafemi Awolowo University. It is also a body propelled towards the “overall development of the budding lawyer.” We stand to add value, contribute to the intellectual expansion and academic advancement of every law student.
